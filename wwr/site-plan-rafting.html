Overview

The Dry Oar Rafting Co. is a an amateur outdoors sports company. It sells maped trips with rented canoes, kayaks, tents, and pre-prepared freeze dried meals. Customers schedule a trip for a specific date and location. They are given all the equipment, food, and maps for their trip. All trips range from a few days to a few weeks in the American Midwest and West Coast.

Purpose

This site is not for advertising, social media is much better for that. Rather this site is a listing of the trips offered. It includes location, distance, estimated time, and price of the trip. Potential customers can pick their trip, schedule it up to 2 years in advance, and pay the total amount through this site.

Audience

The target group is amateur outdoorsman. Fathers and their younger sons, dating or married couples, groups of college students, small scoutsmaster outings. We offer a simple, easy, and safe way to have a outdoor vacation.
